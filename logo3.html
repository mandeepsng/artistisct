Cultural Significance

The logo design celebrates Indian heritage through traditional elements: the lotus represents purity and spiritual awakening, mandala patterns symbolize cosmic order and unity, saffron color connects to spirituality and courage, while paisley motifs honor textile traditions. Each variation maintains cultural authenticity while embracing contemporary design principles.
