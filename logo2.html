Luna Wilde Logo Variations

1. Original Vertical Logo

The main logo with full artistic elements - perfect for headers, business cards, and gallery displays.

Best for: Website headers, business cards, gallery displays

2. Horizontal Layout

Perfect for letterheads, email signatures, and wide banner spaces.

Best for: Email signatures, letterheads, wide banners

3. Simplified Icon

Clean, minimal version for social media profiles, favicons, and small applications.

Best for: Social media, favicons, app icons

4. Text-Only Version

Clean typography-focused version for minimalist applications and when simplicity is key.

Best for: Minimalist designs, professional documents

5. Monogram Badge

Circular badge design perfect for stamps, stickers, and branded merchandise.

Best for: Stamps, stickers, merchandise, packaging

6. One-Line Signature

Ultra-compact version for tight spaces, mobile headers, and watermarks.

Best for: Mobile headers, watermarks, tight spaces

Usage Guidelines

Color Palette

Primary: #d4830a
Secondary: #e49547
Accent: #f4b942
Text: #8b5a2b

Typography

Primary: Playfair Display
Secondary: Inter
Weights: 300, 400, 500

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of one letter around the logo

Minimum Size

Never scale below 60px width for detailed versions, 24px for simplified icon
